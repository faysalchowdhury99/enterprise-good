To enterprise good u have to understand why.

Why do we advocate surpassing our way of life? , We 1st have to establish what is it about our market oriented way of life that makes it worth disposing of. This means looking at what we accept for ourselves when we accept it without question as the way towards life we presuppose.

So what does it mean to be market oriented:

We accept the following:

Agency reduced to want - Want is primary because money is a given, you are not given credit for animating the world, you accept the limitation of your agency as you resolve upon securing lifestyle, or the want of a life as you are a consumer of it. The diminution of creativity is ensured through the way your actions are only ever considered economising, you are compelled to economise, as you have to secure independence from the claim thus necessitating a conservatism. Resolving upon your style of life ensures the vision of the possibility for life is circumscribed to the good of your wants. So you maintain life in the holding pattern of the way it is given.

Time is received as an economic good - time is produced as an economic good such that time becomes a supplement to the means we make of ourselves to produce it. Time considered an economic good is abstracted from “life” such that money is its sign for we say time equals money. There is no outside to the claim our way of life makes on us when we are beholden to time as an economic good, it ensures time disappears through its reification.

Relation reduced to want - want is primary relation you share with other people because the world is accepted as a given for the good of your wants. People are a means to realise your way of styling the supplement you secure, so are subordinated for the good that underwrites your being-in-the-narket; the preeminent good of your time.

Beholden to an unavowed utopianism - your time is the preeminent good you are focused on. As the market for your time does not exist outside you, in the way you determine whether you are means or end, so determine the threshold, or marginality of your time, there is no place for the market for your time. So if there is no place for your time you are beholden to an unavowed utopianism. As there is no outside to your-being-in the- market for your time, you are beholden to considering your time the preeminent good without ever being aware enough to understand how this compromises the places you share.

A new fate….

The significance of our market oriented way of life ensures the world we share is marginal. If we are beholden to an unavowed utopianism because time is an economic good we secure as a supplement then we can never remove ourselves from the claim this has to share ourselves. Our way of life removes ourselves from where we are, as when focused on independence from the claim that works on us, we individualise the pursuit of the good life, as the ideal we aspire to is freedom from. Sharing invariably is a supplement to our securing. Think of having drinks with friends; your time is a supplement you’ve secured to be spent as an economic good, so underwrites the moment, the place you share, for the want of a drink with your friends to be given. There is no outside the claim our way of life has, it is totalising.

We do not accept life need be given this way. This is why to engender a cultural renewal entails potentializing your time beyond the economic good you receive it as to temporalise place for a common surpassing. Creating a cultural dynamic, an ethos, means the security you otherwise individualise becomes common to the culture you share as this surpasses the way life otherwise given. This is the opportunity for life, we would say is the opportunity cost of our way of life, but because we never consider alternatives to our way of life, it is never given as the cost of our actions. To offer an opportunity is necessary for us to see what is open for us in our way towards life. This means we have to see through the gap, or difference in values that is the precondition for there to be any exchange. As we said there is no outside the claim that works on us so it is only through the gap can we potentialise a way toward life that is not beholden to the good of want, the way we are marketed to, or the preeminent good of our time, By harnessing creativity through a different way of giving and receiving, this difference is conducive to releasing the good will money conceals whereby we can start to animate the creation of a world, and so bequeath a legacy worthy of our lives..

The noones

We are the no ones a market oriented way of life presupposes, the abysmal “consumer’ , it is the no ones that will create the world just not the way it is given now. To democratise creativity so engender a demos that is not beholden to any origin, as no-one, literally no one is responsible as we all in our own way in the turns taken animated a new dreaming,a new creation story. The question we need ask is do you want to contribute to this stories telling?
