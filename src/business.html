Beyond the bottom line is the thread of a legacy, let us help find yours!

What story would be worthy of not only your enterprise but the people that support it?

Consider the rise of sustainability if it has taught us anything it is that marketing in a post consumer world will not be done how it is now. This much is certain.

So look beyond the trend.

If consciousness is rising on the planet you need to read through it, to turn yourself inside out and back to front to position yourself on the other side the trend is pointing to

Why ?

You already know “consumers” are people 1st so start treating them as such. You need to approach them not as consumers but as people acknowledging the as yet untapped creativity that lays dormant for the sake of viewing them only ever as potential customers. So you need to paradoxically unmarket the relation. This way you will open your enterprise up to see beyond what is given, perhaps the self limitation you unknowingly accept and so align to a greater future both of you can share when its creation becomes a common goal of a much more deeper relationship.

“64% of millennials say business “have no ambition beyond wanting to make money”

Question everything

Rethink the future, ask questions no one asks such as; where does growth come from in a zero growth world?

What is the cause of meaninglessness in the modern world?

What does buying with ones “values” point at?

Take heed and look at your enterprise with fresh eyes.

There is an opportunity right now that has always been open, an opportunity not defined by a market nor the want that legitmises the average relation humans share in a market oriented way of life.

There is a story, a story Enterprise Good can help business tell. We are here to help you understand this opportunity, that ennobles your enterprise, to guide you through this process that ultimately makes it part of an ecosystem like no other retail ecosystem, and eventually no other economy can compete against.

You are always bigger than the market you service, so start acting like it

The only cause you need to champion is the cynicism you create when treating people as profit centres
